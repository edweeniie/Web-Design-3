Our Beliefs

Music instruction is more important than ever now. It offers students regular interaction with a teacher who is focused only on them, provides an individualized course of study, and gives students a pleasant and engaging activity they can do independently at home. We offer several performance opportunities throughout the year for our students enrolled in private instrumental or vocal instruction. Our recitals are now online and weâ€™ve added virtual recording sessions to our offerings, so our students are keeping their performance skills sharp! Mixed Recitals bring together students of different instruments and teachers to perform for each other, family and friends.

In addition to performances, we offer games, contests, and challenges to keep our students motivated to practice and improve throughout the year. Some of these include Scale Olympics, held concurrently with the summer or winter Olympics, the yearlong Trophy Club, where students strive to memorize and perform 10 pieces, Rhythm Challenge, and the Circle of Fifths theory Game. Many of our students have gathered quite a collection of pins, ribbons, and trophies over the years!Several of our teachers enroll their more motivated students in outside adjudications such as Royal School, MTAC, and National Association of Teachers of Singing, where they can receive outside feedback and progress through a graded system.
