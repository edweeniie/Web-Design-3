Private Lesson Tuition

Tuition for private lessons currently ranges from $136-$160/month for a weekly 30-minute lesson.

Tuition is due on the first lesson of the month, in advance for the month, paid directly to your teacher. They will provide their payment options (Venmo, Paypal, etc.) upon registration. Tuition covers four lessons a month. When a month has five lesson days in it, the fifth is used to balance out a month that has only had three lessons in it due to a holiday or scheduled teacher absence. Tuition is not prorated for missed lessons. We ask for 48-hour notice (illness excepted) in order to schedule a makeup. If sufficient notice is not given for a missed lesson the student forfeits that lesson and tuition is used to hold the spot. Please make your lesson time a priority; if an excessive amount of makeups is required we may not be able to hold a permanent spot for you.
